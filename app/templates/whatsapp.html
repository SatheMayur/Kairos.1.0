1

3

4
Scan the QR code with your WhatsApp phone

Follow these steps on your phone:

1
Open WhatsApp on your phone
2
Tap the three dots ⋮ in the top right corner
3
Tap Settings → Linked Devices → Link a Device
4
Point your phone camera at the QR code on the right

When a candidate replies to your WhatsApp message, the system reads their reply and responds automatically:

YES / Interested
Candidate is marked as Interested. System sends them 3 interview time options and creates an interview slot.
1 / 2 / 3
Candidate picks a slot. System confirms their interview time and updates their status to Interview Scheduled.
NO / Not interested
Candidate is marked as Not Interested. System sends a polite goodbye. No rejection email is sent.
Anything else
System replies asking them to say YES or NO. Their status stays the same.
Unknown number
Message is ignored — no candidate found with that phone number.
Words that count as YES
yes, haan, ok, sure, interested, apply, send, great, confirm, proceed…
Words that count as NO
no, nahi, not interested, busy, already placed, stop, unsubscribe…
